Pay Your Bill Online

Most people associate old and outdated designs for an insurance company. Updating the UI to a more modern treatment was the first big impact. A key anchor for this process was developing consistent patterns for the user. It not only lets the insurance member realize where they are in the flow but also lets them know what they can expect in the next step to complete their payment.

I worked with my manager, Eric Crawford; Ashli Thomas, Pankaj Bhagwat and a team of developers to create the applications.

My Role: Visual Web Designer & Interaction Designer
